<p>
    This is a small project I've made for IHWBD (International Homeware Bangladesh), which is one of the Bangladesh's unique trading company that introduced all kinds of homeware products to the market. It took me around two months (December 2011 – January 2012) to complete. Because I also had to continue my graduation, I hardly managed time for this project. But, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alhamdulillah">Alhamdulillah</a> the project completed within the deadline. Beside the main website I also had to make a separate admin panel, so that they can upload new products and manage, manage user settings, control image slideshow etc.
</p>
