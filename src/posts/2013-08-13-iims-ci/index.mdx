<p>
    I am so happy to announce that the first beta version of IIMS (Inventory &amp; Invoice management system) is launched. Actually it was launched two months ago. IIMS is an easy-to-use, online inventory and invoice management system that also help you manage your customers, employees.
</p>

<h4>Technologies used in this project:</h4>

<ul>
    <li><a href="http://www.codeigniter.com/">CodeIgniter</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://www.telerik.com/kendo-ui">Kendo UI</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://handlebarsjs.com/">HandlebarsJS</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://getbootstrap.com/2.3.2/">Bootstrap 2.3.2</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://www.highcharts.com/">Highcharts</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://fontawesome.io/">Font Awesome</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://www.mysql.com/">MySQL</a></li>
</ul>
